Three layers of scam protection

Fake QR stickers on parking meters are a growing problem across North America. ParkOnce is the only app built to catch them.

Domain verification Every scanned URL is checked against our whitelist of legitimate parking providers before anything opens.
Location cross-check We compare the zone in the QR against our database of known zones at your GPS location. A mismatch triggers a warning.
Sign validation Our OCR reads zone numbers directly from the physical sign and cross-references them with the QR — catching tampering no other app can detect.
